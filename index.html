Gluon.city

Gluon is a business city in the metaverse, independent of most current biases such as nationalities, regions, color, currencies and religion. Anybody can join the city to start a new life with equal opportunities.

Residents will have voice, gestures and access controls similar to real life and can opt to be searchable to their location by others in the city. Popular people can choose different avatars to stay anonymous. Residents can make calls, leave notes and even post letters to each other.

Rule # 1. Stay Anonymous

We believe there is a strong reason why Nakamoto Satoshi, the great visionary, chose to stay anonymous and let his product fight for itself. We recommend to stay anonymous and neutral in the metaverse, don't play any bias in good or bad way.

Life in Gluon

Gluon is a business hub, residents will be doing business deals and transactions while staying totally anonymous to each other. After business, entertainment SEX would be the second best activity because residents can have children.

Residents

Residents can choose to be male or female. Residents will join Gluon with basic and similar traits. Residents can bring in any object from their wallets to put on and use in the Gluon.

Offices

Each resident will receive an office in Gluon city where he can make private business deals, other residents can visit him in the office only after appointment.

Shops

Residents can buy shops to sell their assets to residents, anybody can visit shop and buy items 24/7, no appointments required.

Auditorium

Gluon has an auditorium to do spaces, AMAs and presenting your work and ideas to residents.

Gallery

Gluon features a gallery where new NFTs, assets and metaverse objects can be launched and dropped.These objects can be used in Gluon and in other metaverses.

Cinema

Exclusive launches of Movies and TV Shows for Gluon residents.
