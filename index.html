Gluon.city

Gluon is a business city in the metaverse, independent of most current biases such as nationalities, regions, color, currencies and religion. Ownership, free will, privacy, decentralisation and connectivity with anonymity are the core values of the Gluon City.

Residents will have voice, gestures, access and emotional controls similar to real life and can opt to be searchable to their location by others in the city. Popular people can choose different looks to stay anonymous. Residents can make calls, leave notes and even meet in person after appointment to each other.

Anonymity

We believe there is a strong reason why Nakamoto Satoshi, the great visionary, decided to stay anonymous and let his product fight for itself. Gluon supports anonymity and privacy, we will provide tools and help you to stay anonymous.

Connectivity

Your mobile will be your best companion in Gluon city. You can browse the internet, leave notes, make calls and meet in person after appointment to the other residents of the city.

Life in Gluon

Gluon is a business hub, residents will be doing business transactions, deals and negotiations while staying totally anonymous to each other. After business, limitless and utmost entertainment would be the second best activity.

Residents

Residents can choose to be male or female. Residents will join Gluon with basic and similar traits. Residents can bring in any object from their wallets to put on and use in the Gluon.

Offices

Each resident will receive an office in Gluon city where he can make private business deals, other residents can visit him in the office only after appointment.

Shops

Residents can buy shops to sell their assets to residents, anybody can visit shop and buy items 24/7, no appointments required.

Auditorium

Gluon has an auditorium to do spaces, AMAs and presenting your work and ideas to residents.

Launchpad Gallery

Gluon features a gallery where new NFTs, assets and metaverse objects can be launched and dropped.These objects can be used in Gluon and in other metaverses.

Cinema

Exclusive launches of Movies and TV Shows for Gluon residents.
