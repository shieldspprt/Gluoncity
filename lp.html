Lite Paper

Gluon is a business city in the metaverse, independent of most current biases such as nationalities, regions, colour, currencies and religion. Anybody can join the city to start a new life with equal opportunities. The metaverse introduces a unique communication experience through the combination of both traditional and blockchain mechanics. We believe brain understanding is gearing up, more and more engineering and interfacing with brain in the future is coming. Next generation is less likely to trade off real world for VR and AR experiences, we envision VR future would be much like reading a book. Staying in pods or making moves while wearing headsets is not going to work in the long run, that’s why Gluon is working on voice, access, gestures and emotional controls to mimic the real life in the metaverse city. Residents will have voice, gestures and access controls similar to real life, make calls, leave notes and search each other to their location in the city while staying completely anonymous. People will be recognised from their wallet holdings.


Goals

1. Removing current political and economic biases by providing space to promote merit culture for innovation.

2. Developing tools to mimic real life in the metaverse.

3. Promoting ownership, free will, privacy, decentralisation and connectivity with anonymity.

4. Providing platform for everyone to enhance the metaverse experience for all of us by designing assets and developing functionalities.


Technologies

Gluon is promoting meritocracy based on decentralising tools such as blockchain, try to use and develop open source technologies wherever possible. Mozilla hubs, AWS, Linux, GitHub, Solana, metaplex and sketchfab community are the power house of Gluon. More communities will be formed around the Gluon city to accelerate the development. Open APIs and SDKs will be provided to bring more assets and functionalities to the Gluon city. We are building on Solana, currently the most efficient and robust chain (carbon neutral) and experiencing the highest interest from the developer community.


Project Outline

Gluon is a green city with zero carbon emissions, open for everyone in the world to conduct business and experience utmost entertainment. Gluon features;


Residents: Gluon has male/female characters as residents and visitors. Residents have an office to conduct business and do whatever that want. Visitors can buy and roam around in the city, can not sell anything and no children.

Trade Center: Ski-fi buildings consist of modern offices to conduct businesses privately. Visitors and residents need to have an appointment to meet someone in his office and only possible when he/she is in the metaverse.

Shopping Mall: All offices and residents join with basic avatars and office facilities, later they can decorate by going to shopping mall and buy assets to represent themselves. Shops are open to all, people can visit without any appointment and can buy assets even if the owner is not present in the metaverse.

Entertainment: Entertainment would be second best activity in the Gluon city, residents will be able to experience SEX and have children if they want to.

Launchpad Gallery: An art gallery in the trade centre to launch new NFT and the metaverse assets projects. Residents will be able to mint and collect assets in the metaverse.

Auditorium: Presenting projects, doing AMA’s and spreading ideas to residents and visitors.

Sports Zone: Car racing track, drone flying, shooting club and other sports.

Cinema: A cinema in the Gluon city to launch exclusive media content to the residents and visitors.



Economics

Gluon team will be developing city and its core functions to interact among the assets. Each resident will join Gluon with an office and an avatar. Gluon is a business hub in the metaverse, residents will join to perform business deals and buy/sell their assets. Companies and NFT projects can open up their office and showrooms to interact and show case their products to residents and visitors.

Assets Marketplace: All assets designed and developed for the Gluon city will be traded in the shopping mall available in the metaverse city.

Licenses: Commercial licenses will be sold for different type of business activities.

Facilities: Cinema, shopping mall, trade centre, gallery, sports zone and characters will be sold to investors and partners.

Project office: Projects such as opendao, Solana monkey business, Star Atlas and aurory can open their project offices to show case their incoming assets.

Advertising: Gluon city will have an efficient advertising system for anyone to promote anything in the city.

All investment options will be open to all. Gluon will try not to fall into in the hands of FEW. .
